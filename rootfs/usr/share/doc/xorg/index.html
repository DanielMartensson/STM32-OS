X Strike Force’s documentation

The “X Strike Force” takes care of packaging X.Org for Debian.

 

These documents are shipped in the xserver-xorg metapackage (under /usr/share/doc/xorg), starting with wheezy. They are also available online at x.debian.net, which is an alias for the longer-to-type pkg-xorg.alioth.debian.org.
